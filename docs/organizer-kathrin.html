Organizer Details

Kathrin Plankensteiner

Local Organization

Kathi studied technical mathematics and data analysis at the University of Klagenfurt, Austria. She received her PhD in applied statistics in 2015. During her studies, she worked for KAI (Kompetenzzentrum Automobil- u. Industrieelektronik GmbH). Now she works as a Data Scientist at Julius Blum GmbH and as a research assistant at Digital Factory Vorarlberg, FH Vorarlberg. Her field of research includes reliability testing and analyzing, lifetime modeling, regression analysis, computational statistics, multivariate data analysis, statistical inference, machine learning.

Data Pioneers is a conference on data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence. It's organised for data scientists, data engineers, data science managers and all other data science enthusiasts. Interested? Then join us! We are looking forward to seeing you at the conference.
